About Crawlr

Our Mission

Easy Planning for Everyone

Finding fun things to do can be hard -- especially if you don't know your area.

Crawlr aims to put easy planning back in the hands of the user.

You just need to tell Crawlr where you want to go and what you like to do, and it will find you a fun trip in your area.

Our Product

A Fun Time, Anywhere

We have designed a mathematical model for planning fun routes

This means that not only is Crawlr finding you fun things to do, but that they are optimized for your requests

You can request routes from Crawlr from anywhere on the globe, meaning you can always find the best things to do, no matter where you are
